About Us






About
honey glow.



Based in New York City, we started out as a small family
breakfast business in Queens - everything locally sourced and
made with love. Today, we have over 100 restraunts open in 20
different cities, namely London, Madrid, Singapore, and more. We
have our brunch spot in @thebrunchandtimes and
@the2022bestcafesmagazine.

Our head chef, Louis, was born and raised in New Orleans and
has been cooking since the age of 13. He can cook just about
anything but his specialty is building on classics from home and
infusing raw, bold flavor, continually inspiring and innovating in
the kitchen.
